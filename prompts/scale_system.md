# scale_system

Prepare `reels-automation-agent` for the next phase of growth without abandoning its local-first core.

## Objective

Evolve the system so it can support deeper automation and future real platform integrations.

## Focus Areas

- publish adapter interfaces for real OAuth and upload APIs
- smarter clip scoring or optional LLM-assisted metadata generation
- stronger job orchestration and resumability
- observability and system status reporting
- improved prompt and automation quality for the AI organization layer

## Constraints

- keep the local-first workflow intact
- avoid heavyweight infrastructure unless absolutely necessary
- preserve the single-user creator-tool baseline

## Expected Outcome

- clearer extension points
- cleaner boundaries between product and automation systems
- a repository that can move from local MVP to serious automation without a rewrite
